Growing Cannabis: Basics

Growing cannabis requires attention to light, water, nutrients, and environment.

1. Choosing Seeds

Select seeds based on desired strain (sativa, indica, hybrid), growth difficulty, and effects.

2. Germination

Start seeds in a warm, moist environment until they sprout.

3. Vegetative Stage

Provide 18+ hours of light per day, adequate nutrients, and monitor humidity and temperature.

4. Flowering Stage

Switch to 12 hours light / 12 hours dark cycle to trigger flowering. Watch for male plants and remove them to prevent pollination.

5. Harvesting

Harvest when trichomes are cloudy/milky for maximum potency. Dry and cure buds properly for best flavor and effect.

Tips

  • Use quality soil or hydroponic setups.
  • Maintain proper pH levels (6.0-7.0 in soil).
