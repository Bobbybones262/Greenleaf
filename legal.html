Where Cannabis is Legal

The legal status of cannabis varies widely across countries and even states or provinces within countries.

Countries with Legal Recreational Cannabis

  • Canada: Fully legal nationwide since 2018.
  • Uruguay: First country to legalize recreational use (2013).
  • United States: Legal in multiple states (e.g., California, Colorado, Oregon), but still illegal federally.
  • South Africa: Personal use and cultivation legalized.
  • Mexico: Recreational use decriminalized; legal framework evolving.

Countries with Legal Medical Cannabis

  • Germany, Australia, Israel, Italy, and many more have legalized medical cannabis under regulations.

Places with Strict Prohibition

Many countries in Asia, the Middle East, and Africa maintain strict penalties including jail time for possession or use.

Important Notes

  • Legal age limits vary.
  • Possession limits and regulations differ by location.
  • Always research local laws before use or cultivation.
