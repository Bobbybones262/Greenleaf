Cannabis in History and Society

Cannabis has been used by humans for thousands of years across many cultures.

Historical Use

  • Ancient China and India used cannabis for medicine and spiritual rituals.
  • Hemp cultivation dates back millennia for fiber and textiles.
  • 19th and 20th-century Western medicine included cannabis tinctures and extracts.

Modern Culture

  • Music: Influential in genres like reggae, hip-hop, and rock.
  • Art: Cannabis-inspired art and counterculture movements.
  • Festivals: Events like 4/20 celebrations worldwide.
  • Activism: Advocacy for legalization and social justice.

Who Uses Cannabis?

Usage spans all demographics, with common users ranging from young adults to seniors, for both medical and recreational purposes.
