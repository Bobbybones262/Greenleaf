General Questions

Is cannabis addictive?

Cannabis can be habit-forming for some people, but it is generally considered less addictive than substances like alcohol or nicotine.

How long do the effects last?

Effects typically last 2-4 hours when smoked or vaporized, but edibles can last 6-8 hours or longer.

Can cannabis show up on a drug test?

Yes, THC metabolites can remain in the body for days or weeks depending on frequency of use.

What are the side effects?

Common side effects include dry mouth, red eyes, increased heart rate, and impaired memory or coordination.

Medical Questions

Can cannabis help with pain?

Many patients find cannabis effective for managing chronic pain, but consult a healthcare provider for personalized advice.

Is medical cannabis covered by insurance?

Typically no, as cannabis remains federally illegal in many countries, insurance rarely covers it.
