Types of Cannabis Strains

Cannabis strains generally fall into three main categories:

  • Sativa: Known for uplifting and energizing effects, often used during the day.
  • Indica: Typically has relaxing and sedative effects, popular for nighttime use.
  • Hybrid: A mix of both sativa and indica genetics, tailored effects depending on the specific strain.
