Medical Uses

Cannabis is widely used to treat or alleviate symptoms of various conditions:

  • Chronic pain relief
  • Reduction of nausea and vomiting, especially in chemotherapy patients
  • Appetite stimulation for conditions like HIV/AIDS or cancer
  • Management of epilepsy and seizures
  • Alleviation of anxiety and PTSD symptoms
  • Muscle spasticity in multiple sclerosis

Recreational Uses

Used for relaxation, creativity, socializing, and mood enhancement. Effects vary based on strain and individual tolerance.

Spiritual and Cultural Uses

Historically, some cultures have used cannabis in religious or spiritual ceremonies to induce altered states or connect with the divine.

Industrial Uses (Hemp)

Hemp, a low-THC variety of cannabis, is used in textiles, paper, biofuels, building materials, and more.
